Conference Overview

 

DevCon 2014 is a two-day associate driven conference for all engineers and interaction design experts working across Cerner. Over 5,000 Cerner engineers are invited to the event. DevCon is an opportunity for associates to learn, interact with distinguished leaders in their profession, and discuss significant topics to their specific role.

The conference is intended to acknowledge the work associates do and see it as a craft, exciting other developers and engineers alike to connect and share ideas. The next decade will require our engineers to be even more agile in their approaches, more thoughtful in their design and more in touch with our clients.

"One of my favorite Cerner gatherings."
- Neal Patterson
